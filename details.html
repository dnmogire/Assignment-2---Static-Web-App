About

Bruno Fernandes is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Manchester United and the Portugal national team. He is known for his creativity, vision, and goal-scoring ability from midfield. Since joining Manchester United in January 2020, he has become one of the team's most important players and serves as the club captain.

Fernandes began his career at Italian clubs Novara, Udinese, and Sampdoria before moving to Sporting CP in 2017. His exceptional performances in Portugal earned him a move to Manchester United, where he quickly became a fan favorite. He has won several individual awards and is considered one of the best midfielders in the Premier League.
